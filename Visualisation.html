Drug Regimen by tumor size overview

The purpose of this project is to analyse laboratory test result on mice tumor response when treated with a variety of drug regimens - Capomulin, Ramicane, Infubinol and Ceftamin. The test results were provided in two seperate commas-delimited files that record the tumor development over the course of 45 days.

Matplotlib was used to build a series of plots to showcase the relationships regarding several variables of tumor size vs. drug regimen. Relationships analyzed included: Capomulin, Ramicane, Infubinol and Ceftamin.

Data and visualizations are provided as part of the analysis, including explanations and descriptions of trends and correlations.
